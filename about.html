Hello, I started my journey into photography a couple of years back and since then, I have made large progress in both capturing and perfecting my shots. I have also since started videography and music production. I cover personal photoshoots as well as small events as a freelance photographer. Do email me at tahshkhem@yahoo.com.sg for any business related enquiries.

My gear consists of a Canon Eos 80D with the 18-55mm IS STM as well as a 50mm f1.8 STM along with a reflector and umbrellas. I also make use of Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom and Premiere Pro for post processing.
